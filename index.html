Our Amazing Team

Rajarshi Roy

CEO, CTO and Founder


Rajarshi brings around 20 years of innovation, execution and management experience in software and hardware. He has held senior leadership positions in Intel Corporation, Synopsys, Motorola, Sun Microsystems and Broadcom. He has carried out his doctoral research work at UT Austin, TX after completing BS and MS in Computer Science & Engineering from BESU-IIEST and Jadavpur University. A resident of Austin, TX, Rajarshi is passionate about music, literature, film and theater and also enjoyed playing various sports.

Debarshi Roy

Chief Scientific Adviser and Co-Founder


Debarshi, a PhD from the University of Texas, is a senior scientist and researcher at Mayo Clinic, USA, is an expert in oncology research and has been active in the development of newer therapeutics to treat drug resistant tumors for over a decade. He has got over 10 years of experience in fundamental research in the domain of cancer biology. He has got several awards and published his research works in reputed international journals in the respective field. He is a scientific adviser and idea generator of SOS. Ourside his research work, Debarshi is an accomplished musician and vocalist.

Deepanjan Datta

Chief Architect


Deepanjan brings around 12 years of in-depth research and innovation experience from academia and industry. He has been a prolific researcher in computational nano-electronics, spintronics, embdded systems, IOT and analytics. Deepanjan published his research in various journal and conferences including Nature, IEEE and IOP. He completed his Doctoral research work at Purdue Univ after finishing B Tech in Electrical Engineering from Indian School of Mines.
